Guide

Click a cell to change landscape type. Cycle order: Urban → Forest → Agriculture → Wetland Design landscape configuration that meets ecological targets. Press **Check Plan** to evaluate your design.

Metric Explanation

Forest Cover
Percentage of forest cells within the landscape.
Patch Density (Urban)
Number of urban patches divided by total landscape area. Lower values indicate less fragmentation.
Largest Patch Index (Urban)
Percentage of landscape occupied by the largest urban patch.
Connectivity
Average connectivity of all land-cover classes based on largest patch proportion within each class.
Habitat Suitability
Composite score based on forest cover and landscape connectivity.
